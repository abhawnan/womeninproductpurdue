About Women in Product

Women in Product creates opportunities for students to gain hands-on experience in product management through structured projects, workshops, and events.

We organize team-based product projects that simulate real-world product development, along with skill-building workshops that cover essential product concepts and tools. The club also hosts guest speakers, panels, and networking events to expose members to diverse product roles and industry perspectives.

What We Do

Our initiatives are designed to prepare members for success in product management and technology-driven roles.

Workshops

Interactive sessions covering core product skills including user research, roadmapping, PRDs, metrics, and stakeholder communication.

Project Work

Semester-long team projects that simulate real-world product development from ideation to launch.

Speakers

Guest speakers, panels, and fireside chats with product managers, founders, and industry leaders.
